Welcome

A hands-on research and experimentation course focused on mathematical modeling, data, and computational methods applied to real-world problems. Designed to train students to think and work like researchers, combining quantitative models, programming, and data-driven decision making.

Course overview Meet the instructors

Course Overview

This workshop-based seminar combines quantitative models, programming, and data-driven decision making. Participants identify problems, design models, and implement solutions using tools such as Python, R, optimization models, simulation, machine learning, and data visualization.

Topics

  • Operations research
  • Ethics in data and modeling
  • AI and machine learning techniques
  • Simulation and optimization
  • Data visualization and analytics
  • Transfer of results into practice

Deliverables

Students produce tangible academic or technical output: research posters, papers, code repositories, datasets, or project proposals. All contributions are preserved in an open repository, fostering continuity and collective knowledge across cohorts.
